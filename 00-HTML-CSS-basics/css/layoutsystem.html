Lore of the North
Interesting new work from Amy Rodchester, who was recently commissioned by Lord Whitney to brand a project exploring folklore. The final images were later compiled into a book, which Rodchester also designed.
 
As Lord Whitney utilises the typeface ‘Frontage’ throughout much of their existing communications, Rodchester created a customised version to ‘reflect folklore characteristics’. The final result is pretty special!
 
If you’re a fan I recommend checking out the rest of her portfolio in addition to AlphaPattern, a personal project of hers that explores typography and pattern.
